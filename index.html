The Highly Recommended is an interview podcast hosted by Becky Perry — long-form conversations with the people in your city, your industry, your world, who quietly built something extraordinary.

As much as we respect the wisdom of those who have reached the top, we got tired of the same faces and the same stories on different sofas. Our guests aren't celebrities. They're everyday people who have done something, survived something, or become something that inspires. Close enough to feel real. Far enough to light a fire.

One guest. One story. No fluff.

And soon — you recommend who's next.
